About MelaMarket

Are you tired of shopping in a world that doesn't reflect the beauty and diversity of our community? Say goodbye to bland, one-size-fits-all shopping experiences and hello to MelaMarket!

Our app is specifically designed for BIPOC shoppers and offers a fresh, customized, and community-driven approach to shopping. With features inspired by Pinterest and the option to add your purchases to your own storefront, shopping has never been so personal and fun!

We also use Google Maps API to connect you with local Black-owned businesses, so you can support your community and get the products you need. Our filtering options make it easy for you to find what's relevant and helpful in your region.

Join the MelaMarket revolution and experience shopping like never before. Let's make every purchase a celebration of our community and culture!
